How To Care

Did you know it's easier to kill a cactus plant than it is to grow and care for it?

  • For starters, just don't water it often as it's a succulent (holds/retains water)
  • Not all compost work well for the cactus plant, it needs it's own special compost.
  • Give it some sunshine in spring and summer times and water it atleast once every 2weeks.
  • keep it away in the winter months and don't water it.
