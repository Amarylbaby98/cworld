About Cactus World

We are charity based in the UK. Our art is towards people who love plants but can't maintain them and all those who just love good Art. proceeds go towards Cactus World Organisations all over the world. Your contributions help save alot of cactus from being smuggled. We stock myriads of Cacti and succulent plants in different sizes to suit all. Cacti and succulents are easy going with nature which fits perfectly with the hectic pace of modern life. They add a clean aesthetics and attraction to a home, office environment. We are offer delievery across London.
